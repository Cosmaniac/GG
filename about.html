We are Perspectiv Technologies.

At Perspectiv Technologies, we believe that artificial intelligence is the way of the future. Originally established as a computer manufacturer, we started to explore the uncharted territory of quantum computing. Realizing the potential of quantum computing in hosting an artificial intelligence, our founder, Adam Savage, began to shift from manufacturing to research. We began to develop new methods of constructing deep neural architecture. Over the course of a decade, we developed and refined Eva, the artificial intelligence that defines our company today.

Perspectiv Technologies is always looking for talented and capable individuals to join our team. We offer countless benefits for our team members, such as paid vacations, health insurance, sick leave, work from home, and a free subscription to Eva. If you would like join our team, please contact us.
