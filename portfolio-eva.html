Meet Eva, build: Gold.6

Eva is the Generalized Artificial Intelligence that powers our robots. She’s no average computer. Engineered with a unique neural network, Eva learns and improves under the same basic mechanisms as the human brain. Software algorithms model the neurons found in an organic brain, making Eva the most intelligent machine on the planet.

Project Details

  • The wisdom of millions: Eva constantly extracts information from the internet to further her abilities. With super fast computation speeds, she can process and learn from humanity’s collective knowledge. As a result, Eva can see the world from a human Perspectiv, allowing for continual development of a unique personality.
  • A brain like no other: Eva operates on a quantum computing framework that is fundamentally superior. The circuits in Eva’s servers use qubits, subatomic particles that exist in several states at a time. Our processors are able to calculate billions of possibilities simultaneously. The result? A machine that thinks hundreds of times faster and more efficiently than its conventional counterparts. Eva is not just ones and zeros, but everything in between.
  • All-Natural interaction: Eva may be artificial, but it's hard to ever tell. Dedicated linguistic processing algorithms allow her to analyze word meanings, tone and even body language. A self-updating database of regional phrases and dialects allows Eva to understand figures of speech, accents, and more. Combine this data with lightning-fast processing times, and you get a lag-free, naturally-conversing companion.
  • Effortless teamwork: Eva’s cloud-based, centralized architecture lets her control and manage entire fleets of robotic units. Robotic teamwork becomes intelligent with Eva, as she allows each robot to make individual decisions while still maintaining frictionless communication or hierarchical command with one another. A cloud-based administrative console allows for easy robotic fleet management.
