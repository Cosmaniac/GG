Acquisition and Product Details

How do I access your services?
Access to Eva, our Cloud AI, is given on a subscription. Subscriptions can be purchased monthly or annually. Eva can be accessed through a web portal at no additional cost. For those who want their AI in physical form, a robotic unit, named Arc, is available as a one time purchase. Finance plans are available.
I represent a company, and would like to bulk order.
We appreciate your interest in upgrading your workflow with our solutions. Please directly contact us with any bulk purchase inquiries. We may be able to offer fleet management services, such as robotic storage, maintenance, and more.
What happens when Arc loses connection to Eva?
With Arc’s comprehensive communications array, the chances of losing connection to Eva are very low. However, in the unlikely event that a disconnect occurs, Arc will enter an offline mode with reduced functionality. In this mode, Arc suspends personality simulation, and responds only to a specific set of commands. Arc will automatically connect at the nearest opportunity.

Company Policy

Will Perspectiv collect my data?
Due to the nature of the services we provide, Perspectiv Technologies does collect and process data. This necessary data is collected in accordance with our privacy policy. We take many measures to secure your data, and any diagnostic or observational data is anonymized before upload to our servers.
Are there risks involved with the purchase of a robot?
As with any purchase, unfortunately there are risks. We at Perspectiv Technologies take every effort to minimize potential dangers, but there is the very small possibility of harm to body or property. Our risk control measures are comprehensive, so we believe the risk of purchasing a robot from us is no greater than that of other electronic “smart” devices. All robotic actions and processes are routed through Eva, which has fundamental behavioral laws which restrict violent actions. See our “Risks and Indemnification” page for more details.
Can I limit Eva’s function or access?
Yes. With some exceptions, you may limit Eva’s access to or memory of information through web controls. Arc falls under the same rules, but you may additionally impose “Red areas”, where access is restricted to the robot. Business robots will be managed by a designated administrator.
