ARC: The Flagship Robot

ARC will change the way you look at the future of robotics. Powered by our powerful EvaAI, ARC can understand what you want him to do even before you know. Using revolutionary compression technology, ARC is more compact than all competitors out there. Hop on board the ark that will carry your company to the future with ARC.

Project Details

  • Multi-Day Power: Charge anxiety is a thing of the past with ARC. A graphene battery built into each unit offers unparalleled battery life and charge time. With normal usage, Arc will fully charge itself in 5 hours every 2.5 days. Intelligent charge scheduling means Arc will only charge when everyone else is asleep. An optional wireless power system installed in the home allows for 24/7 operation.
  • A cloud with a silver lining: To ensure ultimate adaptability, ARC units are designed to have a constant and seamless link with the EvaAI cloud. Say goodbye to system updates, model upgrades, and other hassles: It’s all done to EvaAI on our end. To ensure the connection is never dropped, each unit can connect to the cloud via satellite, cellular, wifi, microwave, radio, and wifi frequencies. A unique compression-encryption algorithm enables information to be exchanged lag-free. When all else fails, an offline failsafe mode engages.
  • Like a human, and more: ARC’s design stresses friendliness and convenience. With over 70 actuators controlling face movement and a realistic skin tone, a unit never looks off-putting. A fully featured sensing package gives every robot advanced spatial awareness. Induction motors and a reinforced skeleton enable each unit to lift over 250 lbs and run up to 15 mph for sustained periods of time.
  • Unrivaled Dependability: Take it out in the rain. Or the snow. To a mountaintop, or deep underwater. Whatever the case, Arc is built for ultimate durability. Military grade water seals allow up to 30 meters of full submersion, and an internal climate control system keeps the robot operational from -40F to 150F. Solid state electronics and shock absorbing joints provide good protection against falls and vibration.
  • A personal respect: ARC strives to be human in every aspect, even by respecting personal boundaries. Any collected data is locally encrypted with a quantum entanglement processor before being shared with EvaAI. Boundary markers can be locally set to restrict the robot from entering into private areas, and any stored sensory data can be reviewed and deleted via a web application.
